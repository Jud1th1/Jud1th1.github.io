Art Gallery Tour App

UX Case Study 1

  • Category: UX/UI Design
  • Project date: May 2022- July 2022
  • UX Portfolio Case Study: Art Gallery Case Study
  • Figma Prototype: Figma File

My first Coursera case study started from my love of art and technology. The challenge: designing a novel check-in app for an art gallery. As a lover of art, I envisioned an app that could redefine gallery experiences. This unique project allowed visitors to seamlessly purchase tickets and book tours, enhancing their art exploration. Guided by Coursera's design thinking modules, I dedicated three months to this creative journey. Follow the link above to read about the stages, from ideation to execution, that culminated in a fusion of creativity and technology, to enhance the way we interact with art.
