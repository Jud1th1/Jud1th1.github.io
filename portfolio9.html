Movie Snack App

UX Case Study 2

  • Category: UX/UI Design
  • Project date: July 2022
  • UX Portfolio Case Study: Movie Snack App Case Study
  • Figma Prototype: Figma File

In my second case study through the Google UX Design Certificate program, I took on a project close to my heart: crafting a mid-movie snack ordering app for web and mobile. As a film enthusiast, I empathized with the desire for uninterrupted enjoyment. This inspired a user journey focused on effortless menu browsing, snack selection, and seamless, in-seat purchasing. Join me in exploring how this case study marries innovation with real-life needs, transforming cinema breaks into indulgent experiences.
