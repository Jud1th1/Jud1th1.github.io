BeatWise Rentals, born as a Javascript capstone project, is another testament to my acquired skills. In collaboration with a classmate, we pitched the idea for the ultimate destination for DJ equipment rentals. My contributions to BeatWise Rentals include responsive elements such as a dynamic navbar, mobile-friendly hamburger menu, and filter buttons for efficient equipment access. Fueled by HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the platform seamlessly marries functionality with style. This project showcases my commitment to delivering an exceptional user experience, with continuous plans for optimization and refinement. Explore the live site here.
