Transforming Thanksgiving with TBUX and Radius Church

In collaboration with Tampa Bay User Experience (TBUX) and Radius Church, we embarked on a mission to enhance their Thanksgiving initiatives in St. Petersburg. Our goal was to streamline operations, improve the experience for community partners, volunteers, and recipients, and make a positive impact on the local community. From project inception to design and the launch of a live site for Thanksgiving 2023, I played a key role in this transformative journey. Our efforts combined innovation, teamwork, and impactful design to create a meaningful impact on our community.
