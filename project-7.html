Art Gallery Check In App

  • Category: UX Design
  • Project date: May 2022- July 2022
  • Prototype: Click Here
  • Project Details: https://uxfol.io/p/ba80d2d3/036c416b

"During my participation in the Google Coursera UX program, I designed a check-in app for an art gallery as my first case study. The project presented a unique opportunity for me to combine my passion for art with my UX design skills. As an avid art enthusiast, I was eager to create a solution that would enhance the visitor experience at the gallery.
To bring this project to life, I followed the comprehensive design thinking flow offered by Coursera modules, which guided my work over a period of three months. The outcome of my efforts is a user-friendly and engaging app that simplifies ticket purchasing and tour bookings while adding value to the gallery experience."
