Serenity Now App: Your Oasis for Tranquil Meditation

Serenity Now is a mobile-friendly meditation and relaxation app designed for a seamless user experience. With curated 10-minute meditation sessions, ambient sounds, and calming visuals, the app provides a serene escape. Challenges included integrating video and audio elements using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and the Swiper library. Explore a mindful oasis with Serenity Now App.
