My mission was to craft a vibrant homepage/landing page for Naples, Florida, ensuring a seamless user experience for both desktop and mobile users. The new design boasts a sleek dropdown navigation bar, integrated page links from the existing Naples website, interactive hover-over menu images, and a social media connection with the inclusion of icons. To make Naples more accessible to all, I also incorporated a Google Translate feature in the footer. Additionally, accessibility was a top priority, guaranteeing that screen readers and keyboard users can effortlessly explore the various sections.
