For my final exam project, I embarked on a heartfelt journey inspired by a dear friend's organization. She had briefly put it on hiatus, and I designed this basic site using old images and files I found to rekindle her passion and provide a fresh start.

In JavaScript, I added a dynamic dark mode that offers visual options for user experience. I also implemented a captivating title animation to engage visitors. As a finishing touch, I polished the design by introducing image shadows, refining links, and decluttering the layout. This project not only reinvigorated my friend's organization but also provided me with invaluable hands-on experience in web development. Through creative code editing and customization, I advanced my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, making this project a rewarding and educational journey.
