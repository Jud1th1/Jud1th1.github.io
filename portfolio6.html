WIX Website

I had the pleasure of collaborating with a special client—my cousin—on her exciting venture into the lash business. Using my Front-End skills, I brought her vision to life on Wix, crafting a stylish webpage with features tailored to her needs. The elegant green theme, echoing sophistication and natural beauty, sets the tone, complemented by custom branding for social media, photos, and logos, showcasing my graphic design expertise. An inspiring "About Us" section adds a personal touch to the site, and Wix's user-friendly platform allowed for easy navigation. I seamlessly integrated features like testimonials, cart, and FAQ pages, utilizing Wix's pre-existing elements. Currently in development, MixxiLash is a project I'm immensely proud of as we refine features and gather user testing feedback.
